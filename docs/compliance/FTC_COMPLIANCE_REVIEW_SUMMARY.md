## Specific FTC Considerations Addressed

### 1. Template vs. Professional Service

**Issue:** Templates could be misconstrued as professional services

**Solution:**
- Clear disclaimers that templates are starting points
- Emphasis on professional review requirement
- Not a substitute for professional advice

### 2. Compliance Guarantees

**Issue:** Templates might imply guaranteed compliance

**Solution:**
- Explicit statements: "does not guarantee compliance"
- Professional review required
- Laws vary by jurisdiction
- User assumes responsibility

### 3. Medical/Healthcare Context

**Issue:** Templates used in healthcare contexts require special care

**Solution:**
- Patient safety emphasized in relevant documents
- Medical/clinical expert consultation required
- Regulatory compliance stressed
- Jurisdictional variations noted

### 4. Legal Documents

**Issue:** Templates that look like legal documents

**Solution:**
- Not legal advice disclaimers
- Legal counsel review required
- Court/trial language avoided
- Educational purposes emphasized
